Invoice Types

Two Invoice Formats,
One for Every Transaction

Whether you are selling a single item or multiple products, there is a format designed for your needs.

Single-Item
🛍️

Single-Item Invoice

Best for one product or service. Shows product description, quantity, unit price, and total with full seller and buyer details.

  • ✓One product or service description
  • ✓Quantity × Unit Price = Total
  • ✓Full seller and buyer information
  • ✓Payment method and transaction date
  • ✓QR code for online verification
  • ✓Printable and downloadable PDF
Multi-Item
📦

Multi-Item Invoice

Perfect for sari-sari stores or any transaction with multiple products listed with an itemized breakdown in one invoice.

  • ✓Unlimited product line items
  • ✓Description, Quantity, Unit, and Amount per item
  • ✓Automatic total computation
  • ✓Full seller and buyer information
  • ✓QR code for online verification
  • ✓Printable and downloadable PDF

Invoice Workflow

5 Statuses, Full
Transparency at Every Stage

Every invoice moves through a clearly defined workflow so both the seller and buyer always know exactly where the transaction stands.

📝
Draft
Seller is preparing the invoice
🤝
Negotiating
Shared with buyer for review
⏳
Pending
Agreed — awaiting payment
❓
Unconfirmed
Payment sent, being verified
✅
Finalized
Confirmed + QR code issued
01

Draft

The seller is creating the invoice. It can still be edited freely before sharing with the buyer.

02

Negotiating

Shared with the buyer. Either party can propose changes and discuss terms before agreeing.

03

Pending

Both parties have agreed on all terms. The invoice is now waiting for the buyer to send payment.

04

Unconfirmed

The buyer has submitted proof of payment. The seller is verifying the received amount.

05

Finalized

Payment confirmed. Invoice is permanently locked, QR code generated, and PDF is available for download.
